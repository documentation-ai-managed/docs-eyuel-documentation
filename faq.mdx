## Account and Billing

<ExpandableGroup>

<Expandable title="How do I upgrade my plan?">

Navigate to your account settings. Select the desired plan and confirm payment details. You receive instant access upon successful billing.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Annual plans save 20%. Review features before upgrading.
</Callout>

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="What happens if I exceed storage limits?">

You get notified via email and dashboard alerts. Upgrade your plan or delete unused files to restore full access.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How to cancel subscription?">

Go to billing settings, click "Cancel Plan," and follow prompts. Access remains until the end of your billing cycle.

</Expandable>

</ExpandableGroup>

## Performance and Limits

Eyuel enforces limits to ensure reliability. Review this table for key quotas.

| Feature          | Free Plan | Pro Plan   | Enterprise |
|------------------|-----------|------------|------------|
| Pages            | 50        | 500        | Unlimited  |
| Storage          | 1 GB      | 50 GB      | 1 TB       |
| API Calls/month  | 1,000     | 100,000    | Unlimited  |
| Team Members     | 1         | 10         | Unlimited  |

<Callout kind="alert">
Exceeding limits triggers soft throttling. Monitor usage in your dashboard.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting Basics

<Expandable title="Page not rendering correctly?" default-open="true">

Check for syntax errors like unescaped `<` characters. Use browser dev tools to inspect console logs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Browser" icon="globe">
    Press F12, view Console tab for MDX errors.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="CLI" icon="terminal">
    ```bash
    npx @mdx-js/mdx your-file.mdx --validate
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

</Expandable>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Slow Loading" icon="loader">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Clear Cache" icon="refresh-cw">
        Run `rm -rf .cache` in your project root.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Optimize Images">
        Compress assets under 500KB each.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Limits">
        Verify against the table above.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Build Errors" icon="bug">
    Ensure Node.js version `>=18`. Update dependencies:
    ```bash
    npm update @mdx-js/mdx mdx-components
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

For persistent issues, contact support with error logs and your workspace ID.
